Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025 · MBP – MakeMeTop Business Profile · makemetop.in · Srinagar, Jammu & Kashmir, India

1. Information We Collect

When you use MBP – MakeMeTop Business Profile, we may collect:

  • Account info: Name, email address, and profile photo when you sign up or sign in with Google
  • Business info: Business name, address, phone, WhatsApp, website, description, logo, cover photo, and gallery images you submit for your MBP listing
  • Reviews: Star ratings and written reviews you post on business profiles
  • Ownership claims: Name, email, phone, and message when you request business ownership
  • Usage data: General analytics via Firebase (pages visited, actions taken)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Create and display your MBP listing publicly on our platform and in Google Search results
  • Send you email notifications (welcome, listing approved/rejected, ownership transfer)
  • Process ownership transfer requests between users
  • Provide live chat customer support
  • Improve our platform and fix technical issues
  • Prevent fraud and enforce our Terms of Service

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore) secured with strict security rules — only you can read and edit your own data. Business images are stored on Google Drive with restricted access. We use industry-standard HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.

4. What We Share

Publicly visible: Your business name, address, phone number, description, photos, and reviews are displayed publicly on MBP and indexed by Google — this is the core purpose of your listing.

Never sold: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to advertisers or third parties.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save draft business listings and install preferences. Firebase uses session cookies to keep you signed in. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

6. Your Rights

  • Edit or delete your reviews at any time from the app
  • Edit or delete your MBP listing from your Dashboard
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us via live chat
  • Request a copy of your personal data

7. Children's Privacy

MBP is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, data requests, or account deletion, contact us via live chat in the MBP app or email: support@makemetop.in
