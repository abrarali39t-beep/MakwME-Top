Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025 · Effective for all MakeMeTop (MMT) users

This Privacy Policy applies to MakeMeTop (also known as MMT, MMTBP, MakeMeTop Business Profile, and makemetop.in), operated from Srinagar, Jammu & Kashmir, India.

1. Information We Collect

When you use MakeMeTop, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile photo when you sign up or sign in with Google.
  • Business Information: Business name, address, phone number, WhatsApp number, website URL, business description, logo, and photos that you submit for your listing.
  • Review Data: Star ratings and written reviews you post on business profiles.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, searches performed, and interactions within the app (collected via Firebase Analytics).
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, operating system, and IP address for security and performance purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your MakeMeTop account and business listings
  • Display your MakeMeTop Business Profile (MMTBP) publicly on our platform and in Google Search results
  • Send notifications about your listing status (approved, pending, or rejected)
  • Improve our platform and fix technical issues
  • Prevent fraud and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Respond to your support requests via live chat or contact form
  • Provide premium business listing services when opted into

3. How We Share Your Information

MakeMeTop does not sell your personal data. We may share information in these limited cases:

  • Publicly Listed Business Data: Your business name, address, phone, description, and photos are displayed publicly on our platform and indexed by Google Search to help customers find you.
  • Firebase / Google: We use Google Firebase for authentication, database, and analytics. Data is stored on Google's servers per their privacy policy.
  • Google Drive: Business images are stored via our Google Apps Script integration for image hosting.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) with industry-standard security. Business images are hosted via Google Drive. We implement Firestore Security Rules to restrict unauthorized access.No payment data is stored on our servers. All payments are securely processed via authorized third-party payment gateways.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

MakeMeTop uses browser local storage to save draft business listings so you don't lose your work. We use Firebase's authentication session cookies to keep you signed in. Our service worker caches static assets for offline use. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party trackers beyond Firebase Analytics.

MakeMeTop provides digital business profile and listing services only. We do not guarantee customer acquisition, leads, or business outcomes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your account information at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Delete your own business listings and reviews at any time
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the live chat in the MakeMeTop app or reach out through WhatsApp.

7. Children's Privacy

MakeMeTop is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us immediately.

8. Third-Party Links

Business profiles on MakeMeTop may include links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of MakeMeTop after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
