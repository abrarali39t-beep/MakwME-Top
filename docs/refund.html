Boost Ads Coming Soon

How to Request a Refund

When Premium launches, to request a refund contact us within the eligible window:

  • Live Chat: MBP app → Settings → Help & Live Chat
  • Email: support@makemetop.in — Subject: "Refund Request – [your email]"

Include your registered email, payment date, and reason. We'll respond within 2 business days. Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to your original payment method.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Free MBP listings (no payment was made)
  • Premium plans where account was suspended for Terms violations
  • Boost Ads after they have been displayed
