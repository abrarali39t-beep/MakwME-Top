Who We Are

MBP – MakeMeTop Business Profile is Srinagar's #1 local business directory for Jammu & Kashmir. We help every business — from a small Lal Chowk shop to a Gulmarg hotel — get discovered online and connect with real customers every single day.

What We Offer

📋

Free MBP Listing

Create your MakeMeTop Business Profile completely free. No credit card, no hidden charges. Your MBP goes live within 24 hours.

🔍

Google-Indexed Profiles

Every MBP is indexed on Google Search with Schema markup — your business name, address, phone and reviews appear in search results for Srinagar & J&K.

⭐

5-Star Customer Reviews

Customers leave 5-star reviews on your MBP. Real reviews from real customers help build trust and attract more visitors to your business.

📞

Direct Contact Buttons

Call, WhatsApp and Website buttons on every MBP profile. Customers reach you directly in one tap — no middlemen.

🏢

Ownership Management

Business owners can claim and manage their MBP. If someone else created your listing, use "Own This Business" to request ownership and take control.

📲

Install as App (PWA)

Install MBP on your home screen — works offline, loads instantly, and feels like a native app. Go to Settings → Download App.

🗺️

QR Code for Every MBP

Every business profile gets a unique QR code. Print it on your shop, visiting card or menu — customers scan and reach your MBP instantly.
